### PI Spotlight

**Bio:**
Dr. Samuel Achilefu is the inaugural Chair of Biomedical Engineering at UT Southwestern and holds the Lyda Hill Distinguished University Chair. Elected to both the National Academy of Engineering and the National Academy of Medicine, his lab develops optical imaging technologies that change how cancer is diagnosed and treated — in the operating room and beyond.
